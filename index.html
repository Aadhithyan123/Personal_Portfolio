      Problem statement:
      The problem addressed by this project is the need for a website that helps users plan their diet based on personalized inputs and also provides an online food ordering service. Users require a simple interface to input their age, weight, height, meals per day and diet preference (vegetarian or non-vegetarian) to receive tailored diet suggestions. Additionally, the application should allow users to browse a menu of dishes, view nutritional information, and place orders for food online, providing an easy way to manage both their dietary needs and food orders. The Website must be responsive, providing a seamless experience across various devices, and include features like input validation, order confirmation, and user-friendly navigation to ensure both functionality and ease of use

      Objective of the Project:
      Users can input their age, weight, height, meals per day, and diet preferences to receive personalized diet suggestions The diet suggestions are based on their diet preferences (vegetarian or non-vegetarian).The form collects necessary data to generate tailored health and nutrition recommendations.
